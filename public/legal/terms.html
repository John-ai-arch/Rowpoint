The service

RowPoint records rowing workouts, connects to compatible fitness equipment over Bluetooth, and provides training analytics, plans, predictions, and simulations. You need an account with a verified email address to use it.

Not medical advice

Everything RowPoint shows you — readiness scores, training recommendations, predictions, race simulations — is decision support computed from your training data. It is not medical advice. Consult a physician before starting or changing an exercise program, and stop training if you feel unwell.

Predictions and simulations

Predicted times, probabilities, and simulated races are model estimates with stated uncertainty. Real performance depends on factors no model captures. RowPoint states its assumptions and confidence; it makes no guarantees.

Your responsibilities

  • Do not attempt to access other users' data or disrupt the service.

Your content and data

Your training data remains yours. You can export it or delete your account at any time from Settings. Team and group content you posted may remain visible to those groups after you leave, minus your identity, where removal is impossible.
