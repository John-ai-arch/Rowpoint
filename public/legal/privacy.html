RowPoint Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026 · Back to RowPoint · Terms of Service

RowPoint is a rowing training application. This policy describes exactly what data the app stores and why. There is no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no sale of data.

What we store

  • Account data: your email address, display name, and a salted password hash (scrypt). Optional profile details you choose to enter (weight, goals, demographics) power training features.
  • Training data: workouts, splits, heart-rate data from sensors you connect, wellness check-ins, plans, races, and the derived training metrics the app computes from them (your "athlete state", predictions, simulations).
  • Social content: what you post to teams and groups you join.

Bluetooth

Bluetooth is used only to connect to your rowing machine and heart-rate sensors. It is never used to determine your location and no Bluetooth information leaves your device except the workout data you save.

Research contributions (optional)

If research contribution is enabled in Settings, a pseudonymized copy of workout metrics (never your name or email) enters an aggregate research dataset. The pseudonym is derived with a separate secret so the research data cannot be linked back to accounts. You can opt out at any time; opting out stops all future contribution immediately. Deleting your account deletes your research rows too.

AI features

Coaching text may be generated by an AI model using your training summary. Your email and name are not included in AI requests. All computational engines (predictions, simulations, optimization) run on RowPoint's own server.

Your rights

  • Export: download your data as CSV from Settings at any time.
  • Deletion: delete your account from Settings; this permanently removes your account, workouts, social content, and research contributions.
  • Consent: research and experiment participation are separate, explicit opt-ins you can change at any time.

Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored on access-controlled infrastructure with encrypted backups. Sessions are signed tokens; passwords are never stored in readable form.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: contact the operator of the RowPoint instance you signed up on.
